Creative Process

If you have creative, we’ll produce, direct and/or edit it. If you don’t have creative, we’ll learn about your brand, get to know your objectives, come up with a range of ideas, write scripts, draw storyboards, make animatics, and then we’ll produce, direct and/or edit it.

Production

The creative is done, so what now? Producers, directors, actors, gear, stunts, locations, schedules, logistics, wardrobe etc, etc, etc. There are a large, (but finite) amount of details that go into a production. We’re experts at every step. We’ll make this process positive for you.

Post Production

Whether we shot the footage, you provided it, we’re creating an animated or stock footage piece, we have the experts that edit, add sound and music, color-correct, add titles, graphics and effects.

Distribution

We don’t do this, per se… but we have incredible partners who know exactly how to get your content in front of your audience. They’re amazing at what they do, (and frankly a little scary with how much they know about targeting specific customers). We won’t build content if you don’t have a strategy to get it to your audience. (That’s a recipe for failure and we don’t like failure). So if you need a media agency, we’ll introduce you.
